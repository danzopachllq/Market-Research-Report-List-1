<p><p>The thermosyphon receiver market is segmented into two primary types: direct and indirect. In a direct market, the thermosyphon system transfers heat directly from the collector to the working fluid, ensuring efficient energy conversion. Conversely, the indirect market utilizes a heat exchanger, where the heat from the thermosyphon is transferred to a secondary fluid before reaching the end-use application. This distinction impacts efficiency, installation complexity, and thermal performance, catering to varied applications in solar energy systems and heating solutions.</p></p>
