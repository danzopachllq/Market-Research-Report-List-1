<p><p>Electricity transmission towers serve to facilitate the transport of electrical energy over long distances. The market is categorized into two main types: AC (Alternating Current) and DC (Direct Current). AC transmission is widely used due to its ability to easily transform voltage levels, making it efficient for short to medium distances. In contrast, DC transmission is favored for long-distance transmission due to lower energy losses and reduced grid instability, especially in high-capacity projects and undersea cables. Both types are crucial for a reliable electricity supply.</p></p>
