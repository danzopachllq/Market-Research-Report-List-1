<p><p>The airborne surveillance market comprises three primary types: LiDAR, radar, and imaging systems. LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) uses laser light to measure distances and create high-resolution maps, offering detailed topographical data. Radar employs radio waves to detect and track objects, making it valuable for aviation and military applications. Imaging systems utilize cameras and sensors to capture visual data, aiding in reconnaissance and monitoring. Together, these technologies enhance situational awareness across various sectors, including defense, security, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
