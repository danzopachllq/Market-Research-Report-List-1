<p><strong>The Galvanized Steel Bar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar</li><li>Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The galvanized steel bar market includes two primary types: conventional hot-dip galvanized rebar and continuous hot-dip galvanized rebar. Conventional hot-dip galvanized rebar is produced by immersing steel in molten zinc, providing corrosion resistance suitable for various construction applications. In contrast, continuous hot-dip galvanized rebar is manufactured through a continuous process, ensuring a uniform zinc coating and enhancing production efficiency. Both types offer durability and protection against rust, making them ideal for infrastructure projects and exposed environments.</p></p>
