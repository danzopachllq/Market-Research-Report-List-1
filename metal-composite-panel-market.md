<p><p>Metal Composite Panels (MCPs) are advanced building materials consisting of two thin layers of metal enclosing a non-metal core, typically made from materials like polyethylene or mineral-filled thermoplastics. These panels are favored in construction and architecture for their lightweight nature, durability, aesthetics, and thermal insulation properties. Commonly used in facades, interior applications, and signage, MCPs offer versatility and design flexibility.</p><p>The Metal Composite Panel Market is set to experience robust growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 6.3% during the forecast period.
