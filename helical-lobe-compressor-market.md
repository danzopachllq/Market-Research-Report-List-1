<p><p>The helical-lobe compressor market is categorized into three main types: oil-injected, oil-free, and liquid injection compressors. Oil-injected compressors utilize oil for lubrication and cooling, enhancing efficiency and reducing wear. Oil-free compressors operate without lubrication, making them ideal for applications requiring high purity air, such as food and pharmaceuticals. Liquid injection compressors incorporate a liquid, typically water or refrigerant, to facilitate cooling and lubrication, offering a balance between efficiency and cleanliness while managing heat effectively in demanding environments.</p></p>
