<p><ul><li>Computer-Based Fire Modeling</li><li>Structural Response Modeling</li><li>Modelling of Fire Protection System Response</li><li>Explosion Hazard Assessment</li><li>Preliminary Hazard Assessment (PHA)</li><li>Hazard and Operability Studies (HAZOP)</li><li>Failure Modes and Effects Analysis (FMEA)</li><li>Event Tree Analysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fire Hazard Assessment market encompasses various analytical approaches to evaluate fire risks. Computer-Based Fire Modeling simulates fire behavior in environments. Structural Response Modeling assesses how structures react during a fire. Modeling of Fire Protection System Response analyzes system effectiveness. Explosion Hazard Assessments focus on potential explosive events. Preliminary Hazard Assessments (PHA) identify initial risks, while Hazard and Operability Studies (HAZOP) explore operational safety. Failure Modes and Effects Analysis (FMEA) evaluates potential system failures, and Event Tree Analysis examines the consequences of initiating events in fire scenarios.</p></p>
