<p><strong>The Industrial Gearmotor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Right-angle Gearmotor</li><li>In-line Gearmotor</li><li>Parallel Shaft Gearmotor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial gearmotor market is segmented into three main types: right-angle gearmotors, in-line gearmotors, and parallel shaft gearmotors. Right-angle gearmotors provide compact designs with power transmission at 90 degrees, ideal for space-constrained applications. In-line gearmotors feature a straight alignment for efficient power delivery, often used in high-speed applications. Parallel shaft gearmotors offer a compact design with side-by-side configuration, suitable for high torque and low-speed operations. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and application requirements.</p></p>
