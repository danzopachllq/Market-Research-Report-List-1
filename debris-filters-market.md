<p><p>Debris filters are categorized into W Type and V Type markets based on their design and application. W Type debris filters feature a compact, dual-layer structure that effectively captures larger debris while maintaining high flow rates, making them suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, V Type filters utilize a single-layer, V-shaped design that enhances debris capture efficiency, particularly in environments with fine particulates. Both types are essential for maintaining system integrity and preventing damage in fluid systems.</p></p>
