<p><p>The Split System Heat Pump Water Heater market includes two main types: Air Source Heat Pumps and Water Source Heat Pumps. Air Source Heat Pumps utilize ambient air to transfer heat, making them efficient for moderate climates and widely used in residential settings. In contrast, Water Source Heat Pumps draw heat from nearby water bodies, offering higher efficiency in specific geographical areas. Both systems provide energy-efficient heating solutions, contributing to reduced energy costs and environmental impact in water heating applications.</p></p>
