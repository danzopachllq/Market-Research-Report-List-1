<p><p>The solvent-based pressure label market is primarily categorized into release liner labels and linerless labels. Release liner labels feature a backing that provides easy peeling during application, often used for product labeling and branding. In contrast, linerless labels eliminate the backing, allowing for more efficient use of material and reduced waste, making them ideal for high-speed applications. Both types cater to various industries, offering versatility and adaptability based on specific labeling requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
