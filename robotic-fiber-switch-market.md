<p><ul><li>Singlemode Robotic Fiber Switch</li><li>Multimode Robotic Fiber Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Robotic Fiber Switch market is segmented into two main types: Singlemode Robotic Fiber Switch and Multimode Robotic Fiber Switch. Singlemode switches are designed for long-distance data transmission using a single light path, making them ideal for telecom applications and long-haul networks. In contrast, Multimode switches utilize multiple light paths for shorter distances, commonly used in local area networks and data centers. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to varying bandwidth and distance requirements in modern fiber optic networks.</p></p>
