Cannon snowmakers, larger in scale, utilize a powerful fan to create snow over a broader area, making them suitable for larger slopes. Vehicle-mounted snowmakers combine mobility with snow production, allowing for efficient snow coverage across varied terrains, enhancing versatility in snow management.</p></p>
