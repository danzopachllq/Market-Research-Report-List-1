<p><p>The Sapphire Ball Lenses market includes two main types: Half Ball Lenses and Full Ball Lenses. Half Ball Lenses have a flat base and a semicircular shape, making them ideal for applications requiring light collimation or beam focusing. Full Ball Lenses possess a complete spherical shape, providing uniform light transmission and are typically used in applications such as optical devices and sensors. Both types leverage sapphire's durability and optical clarity, serving industries like telecommunications, electronics, and medical technology.</p></p>
