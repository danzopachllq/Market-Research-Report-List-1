<p><p>The bioremediation technology market encompasses various approaches for environmental cleanup. Bioreactors facilitate controlled microbial processes to degrade pollutants. Biostimulation enhances natural microbial activity using nutrients or amendments. Bioaugmentation introduces specialized microorganisms to improve degradation efficiency. Fungal remediation utilizes fungi for pollutant breakdown, while phytoremediation employs plants to absorb or detoxify contaminants from soil and water. Land-based treatments focus on soil remediation techniques for restoring contaminated sites. Together, these methods contribute to sustainable environmental management and pollution control.</p></p>
