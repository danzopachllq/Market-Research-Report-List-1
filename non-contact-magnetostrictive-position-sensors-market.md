<p><p>Non-contact magnetostrictive position sensors are essential in various industrial applications for precise position measurement. The market can be categorized into two types: analog and digital. Analog type sensors provide continuous output, delivering real-time position data, ideal for applications requiring smooth measurements. Conversely, digital type sensors offer discrete output signals, resulting in binary or multi-bit data representation, which is suitable for systems needing precise positional encoding. Both types utilize magnetostrictive technology to measure linear or rotary positions without physical contact.</p></p>
