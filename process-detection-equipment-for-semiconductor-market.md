<p><p>Process detection equipment in the semiconductor market is essential for ensuring operational efficiency and product quality. Front track detection involves monitoring inline processes for immediate feedback during wafer fabrication, helping detect defects early. In contrast, back track detection focuses on analyzing output and final products to identify issues arising from previous steps in the manufacturing process. Both methods are crucial for maintaining high standards in semiconductor production and facilitating prompt corrective actions to optimize yield and performance.</p></p>
