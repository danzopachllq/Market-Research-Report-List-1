<p><ul><li>"Fluorescence"</li><li>"Phosphorescence"</li><li>"Thermally ActivatedDelayed Fluorescence"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED small molecule light-emitting materials market comprises three main types: fluorescence, phosphorescence, and thermally activated delayed fluorescence (TADF). Fluorescent materials emit light quickly when excited, suitable for lower efficiency applications. Phosphorescent materials, on the other hand, harness triplet state excitons to prolong light emission, offering higher efficiency and brightness. TADF materials bridge the gap by using both singlet and triplet excitons, enhancing efficiency while enabling color tuning. Each type serves distinct application needs in display and lighting technologies.</p></p>
