<p><p>The electrochromic smart glass market comprises four primary types: tinted, clear, colored, and opaque. Tinted glass adjusts its shading to reduce glare and control solar heat gain, enhancing energy efficiency. Clear glass offers the advantage of transparency while enabling adjustable light transmission. Colored electrochromic glass provides aesthetic options and visual privacy while maintaining functionality. Opaque smart glass transitions from transparent to frosted, ensuring privacy in various settings. Each type caters to specific needs, ranging from energy efficiency to design flexibility.</p></p>
