<p><p>Pump as Turbine (PAT) technology utilizes existing pumps to generate electricity in hydropower systems. The market can be categorized into on-grid and off-grid systems. </p><p>On-grid PAT systems connect to the main electrical grid, allowing generated electricity to be sold or used to offset consumption. In contrast, off-grid PAT systems are standalone, serving remote areas or applications where grid access is unavailable. Both types capitalize on surplus water flow, enhancing energy efficiency and sustainability in hydropower operations.</p></p>
