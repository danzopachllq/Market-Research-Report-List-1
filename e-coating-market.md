<p><strong>The E-coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cathodic</li><li>Anodic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The E-coating market is primarily divided into two types: cathodic and anodic. Cathodic E-coating utilizes a negatively charged process, where the substrate acts as the anode, leading to superior corrosion resistance and a thicker coating. In contrast, anodic E-coating uses a positively charged process, resulting in a more uniform finish but typically less corrosion resistance. Both types are widely used in various industries, including automotive and appliances, to provide durable and protective coatings.</p></p>
