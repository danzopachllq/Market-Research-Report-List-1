<p><p>In the rigging system market, there are two primary types: manual and automatic. Manual rigging involves human operators controlling and adjusting equipment, offering greater flexibility and adaptability for specific tasks but requiring more labor and skill. In contrast, automatic rigging employs advanced technology and software to manage systems with minimal human intervention, providing efficiency and precision. While manual rigging is beneficial for intricate or customized setups, automatic rigging excels in speed and consistency for large-scale operations.</p></p>
