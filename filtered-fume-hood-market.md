<p><strong>The Filtered Fume Hood Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Laminar Airflow Fume Hood</li><li>Conventional Fume Hood</li><li>By-Pass Fume Hood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The filtered fume hood market comprises several key types. Vertical laminar airflow fume hoods provide unidirectional airflow, ensuring minimal contamination in sensitive processes. Conventional fume hoods utilize exhaust fans to draw contaminants away from the workspace but rely heavily on a constant supply of air. By-pass fume hoods feature air slots that allow airflow to bypass obstacles, offering enhanced safety and energy efficiency. These variations cater to different laboratory needs, balancing safety, contamination control, and operational efficiency.</p></p>
