<p><p>The construction drone market is primarily divided into fixed-wing and rotary-wing drones. Fixed-wing drones utilize a wing structure to achieve long flight durations and cover extensive areas, making them suitable for large site surveys and mapping. Conversely, rotary-wing drones, commonly known as quadcopters, offer vertical take-off and landing capabilities, enabling them to navigate tight spaces and capture detailed aerial imagery. Both types play vital roles in enhancing efficiency, safety, and accuracy in construction projects, from site inspection to monitoring progress.</p></p>
