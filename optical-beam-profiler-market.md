<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Optical Beam Profiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Camera Based</li><li>Scanning Split</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical beam profiler market is primarily segmented into camera-based and scanning split beam profilers. Camera-based profilers utilize high-resolution cameras to capture and analyze the intensity distribution of laser beams in real-time, offering quick and precise measurements. In contrast, scanning split beam profilers employ mechanical components to sequentially measure the beam profile as it scans across a detector, providing detailed spatial information. Both types cater to diverse applications in research, telecommunications, and manufacturing, ensuring accurate beam characterization.</p></p>
