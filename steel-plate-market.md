<p><p>The steel plate market primarily includes hot rolled and cold rolled steel plates, each serving distinct applications. Hot rolled steel plate is produced at high temperatures, making it more malleable and suitable for construction and heavy machinery. It typically features a rough surface and is less precise in thickness. In contrast, cold rolled steel plate undergoes processing at room temperature, ensuring tighter tolerances and a smoother finish, making it ideal for automotive parts and appliances requiring enhanced durability and aesthetics.</p></p>
