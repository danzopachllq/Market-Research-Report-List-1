<p><ul><li>Non-Absorbable</li><li>Absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthopedic connectors market is categorized into non-absorbable and absorbable types. Non-absorbable connectors are designed to provide long-lasting support and stability, making them ideal for permanent applications, such as in bone fixation and joint reconstruction. In contrast, absorbable connectors are crafted to dissolve within the body over time, eliminating the need for a second surgical intervention. They are commonly used in procedures where temporary support is needed, allowing for natural tissue healing and integration.</p></p>
