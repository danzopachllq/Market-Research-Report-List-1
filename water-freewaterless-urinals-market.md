<p><p>The waterless urinals market comprises several types designed to conserve water and reduce waste. Liquid sealant cartridges contain a special liquid that creates a seal over the drain, allowing urine to pass while preventing odors. Membrane traps utilize a flexible membrane to block sewer gases while allowing liquids to flow through. Biological blocks incorporate enzymes that break down waste and neutralize odors, while mechanical balls utilize floating balls to create a barrier against odors, effectively sealing the drain.</p></p>
