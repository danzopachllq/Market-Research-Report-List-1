<p><p>The RF/Microwave ceramic capacitors market is primarily divided into two types: Class 1 and Class 2 capacitors. Class 1 ceramic capacitors offer high stability and low dissipation factors, making them ideal for precision applications where temperature stability is critical. In contrast, Class 2 capacitors provide higher capacitance values with a good balance of performance and cost but exhibit more variation with temperature and voltage. Both types are essential in RF and microwave applications, catering to diverse electronic needs.</p></p>
