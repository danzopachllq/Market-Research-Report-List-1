<p><p>The smart solar street lights market can be divided into two main types: standalone and grid-connected systems. Standalone solar street lights operate independently, using solar panels, batteries, and LED technology to provide illumination without relying on traditional power sources. They are ideal for remote areas and locations lacking grid access. In contrast, grid-connected solar street lights integrate with the existing power grid, allowing for supplementary energy usage while offering enhanced functionality through smart features like remote monitoring and control.</p></p>
