<p><p>The vegetarian-based capsules market is primarily categorized into three types: starch, pullulan, and others. Starch capsules are derived from natural plant sources, offering a biodegradable option that is suitable for various dietary needs. Pullulan capsules, made from fermented tapioca, provide high stability and oxygen barrier properties, making them ideal for sensitive formulations. Other types may include materials like hydroxypropyl methylcellulose (HPMC) or gellan gum, catering to diverse consumer preferences for vegetarian and vegan products in the nutraceutical and pharmaceutical sectors.</p></p>
