<p><p>The Arsenic and Phosphorus Analyzer market consists of various analytical techniques. Inductively Coupled Plasma Mass Spectrometry (ICP-MS) provides highly sensitive detection of trace elements, including arsenic and phosphorus, through mass analysis. Inductively Coupled Plasma Optical Emission Spectroscopy (ICP-OES) offers simultaneous multi-element analysis by measuring emitted light from excited atoms. Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy (AAS) quantifies elements by measuring absorbed light, while Photomultiplier Tubes (PMTs) are used in fluorescence and nuclear counting applications, enhancing sensitivity in element detection.</p></p>
