<p><p>Low Molecular Weight Heparins (LMW Heparins) are anticoagulant medications derived from standard heparin that exhibit a lower molecular weight. They are primarily used in the prevention and treatment of thromboembolic disorders such as deep vein thrombosis and pulmonary embolism. The advantage of LMW Heparins lies in their improved bioavailability, predictable pharmacokinetics, and reduced risk of heparin-induced thrombocytopenia compared to unfractionated heparin.</p><p>The LMW Heparin Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.2% during the forecast period.
