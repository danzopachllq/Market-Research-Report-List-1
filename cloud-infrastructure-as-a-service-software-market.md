<p><p>The Cloud Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) market can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based IaaS involves providing virtualized computing resources over the internet, offering scalability, flexibility, and reduced maintenance costs for users. In contrast, on-premises IaaS entails deploying infrastructure within an organization's physical location, allowing greater control, security, and compliance but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing management. Each type addresses different business needs, preferences, and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
