<p><p>The bicycle lifts market features two main types: wall-mounted and ceiling-mounted systems. Wall-mounted lifts are attached to vertical surfaces, providing a space-efficient solution for garages or small apartments, enabling easy access and storage. Ceiling-mounted lifts, on the other hand, suspend bicycles from overhead structures, utilizing vertical space and allowing for maximum floor clearance. Both types cater to different storage needs, offering convenience and organization for cyclists while optimizing limited space in urban living environments.</p></p>
